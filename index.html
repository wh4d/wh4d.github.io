About me

I started scripting in Roblox Studio 8 years ago, mostly by taking other people's games apart to see how they worked and then rebuilding the systems myself. Everything on this page, the UI, the meshes, the textures, the animations, was built the same way: for my own projects first, long before I ever thought about taking on client work.

I'm opening that work up now. I'm taking commissions and looking at part-time or full-time scripting roles, and I'd rather show you three finished systems than list a page of skills.

Why me?

I keep code modular and documented, so a project doesn't turn into a black box for the next person who opens it. The idea is that even if I stop working on a project, the studio is still able to work on it no matter what, plus it allows for smoother development overall.

I've worked across a few different genres now (collection games, combat, tool-based systems) so I pick up new mechanics quickly instead of needing everything spelled out. Tell me what's broken or what's missing, and I'll get you a working build fast.
